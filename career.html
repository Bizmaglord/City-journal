Join Our Team at City Journal

Welcome to City Journal, where we curate the latest trends in news, pop culture, world events, fashion, books, art, and the art of living. If you're passionate about these subjects and have a knack for storytelling, we want you to be a part of our creative journey.

Why Work with Us?

At City Journal, we believe in the power of diverse perspectives and storytelling that resonates with our readers. Joining our team means becoming an integral part of a vibrant and dynamic environment. We value innovation, curiosity, and the drive to bring compelling narratives to life.

What We Look For

  • Passion for Trends: We're seeking individuals who share our excitement for staying ahead of the curve. If you have a keen interest in the latest trends in news, pop culture, fashion, and more, you'll feel right at home.
  • Creative Expression: Whether you express yourself through words, visuals, or a combination of both, we welcome diverse forms of creative expression. Bring your unique voice and perspective to our pages.
  • Quality Storytelling: City Journal is committed to delivering top-notch content. If you're dedicated to producing well-researched, engaging, and thought-provoking pieces, we want you on our team.

Perks of Joining City Journal

  • Dynamic Work Culture: Experience a collaborative and dynamic work environment that encourages creativity and innovation.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for trends, culture, and storytelling. Build valuable connections within the industry.
  • Global Impact: Your work at City Journal will have a global reach. Contribute to shaping conversations on a diverse range of topics that resonate with audiences worldwide.

How to Apply

If you're ready to be a part of a team that explores the pulse of trends and culture, we'd love to hear from you. Send your resume, portfolio, and a brief cover letter telling us why you're the perfect fit for City Journal to career@cityjournal.com
